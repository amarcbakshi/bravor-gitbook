# Product Vision

## The Idea

Every time civic capacity surges — after an election, a court ruling, a policy crisis — skilled professionals look for ways to contribute. They find donation links, petition forms, and volunteer sign-ups that don't use their expertise. An attorney who could review a nonprofit's contract instead gets asked to phone bank. A media strategist who could build a press response instead gets directed to a rally. The gap between what professionals can offer and what the ecosystem can absorb is structural — and it costs the movement thousands of hours of high-value work every week.

Bravor closes this gap with an AI-enabled matching platform that treats skilled volunteering as infrastructure, not charity.

## The Product

The platform has two onboarding flows and one matching engine:

- **Contribute your skills.** A conversational AI assistant interviews you — capturing profession, areas of expertise, availability, preferred causes, and contribution style. It builds a rich profile from the conversation, not a form. Once verified, you see a feed of matched opportunities sorted by relevance to your skills and schedule.

- **Post a need.** Organizations onboard through the same conversational interface, articulating specific needs — contract review, financial modeling, press strategy — along with urgency, estimated hours, and deadline. The system matches them with qualified professionals in hours, not weeks.

- **Match, work, track.** Each task has a clear lifecycle: Posted → Matched → In Progress → Completed → Feedback. Both sides rate the experience. The matching engine learns from every interaction.

## Core Capabilities

- **AI conversational onboarding** — builds detailed profiles through natural dialogue, not form fields. Captures nuance (specialization within law, comfort with public-facing work, schedule constraints) that forms miss.

- **Dynamic skill matching** — routes requests to professionals based on expertise alignment, availability, cause area, and contribution history. Gets smarter with each match.

- **Vetted organization network** — every partner organization is verified for mission alignment, organizational capacity, and track record. Contributors know their time goes to credible, high-impact partners.

- **Trust and verification** — professional credentials, LinkedIn profiles, portfolio links, and code of conduct agreements. Both sides know who they're working with.

- **Impact tracking** — connects volunteer contributions to organizational outcomes. Contributors see the dollar value of their professional services delivered; organizations see capacity scaled without recruitment overhead.

## Why Now

Three forces make this viable today:

- **Unprecedented professional mobilization.** The post-2024 environment has created the largest cohort of skilled professionals seeking meaningful civic engagement in a generation. The demand exists — the infrastructure doesn't.

- **AI makes matching feasible at scale.** Conversational onboarding and intelligent skill-to-need routing are now cost-effective. Five years ago this platform would have required an army of human matchmakers.

- **The ecosystem needs it.** Pro-democracy organizations are scaling their work while facing staffing crises. The gap between what they need and what they can hire has never been wider.
