# Live Prototype

## Try the platform

A working prototype of Bravor is live and available for testing:

**[https://bravor-impact-hub.base44.app](https://bravor-impact-hub.base44.app)**

This prototype demonstrates the core matching experience — onboarding, task posting, matching, and impact tracking. It is a functional application, not a mockup.

## How to access

1. Visit [bravor-impact-hub.base44.app](https://bravor-impact-hub.base44.app)
2. Click **Sign Up** to create an account with your email
3. Choose your role: **Contributor** (professional volunteer) or **Organization** (frontline nonprofit)
4. Complete the AI chat onboarding — a conversational assistant will guide you through profile creation
5. Explore the dashboard: browse matched opportunities, post tasks, or review impact metrics

No invitation code is required. The prototype is open to select partners for evaluation.

## What to look for

**If you're a potential funder:**
- The AI onboarding flow — how it captures professional nuance through conversation rather than forms
- The matching engine — how it connects specific skills to specific needs
- The impact dashboard — how it quantifies the dollar value of professional services delivered

**If you're a potential project lead:**
- The two-sided architecture — how both contributor and organization experiences are designed
- The task lifecycle — how work moves from Posted → Matched → In Progress → Completed → Feedback
- The admin panel — how the platform is managed and moderated

**If you're a potential partner organization:**
- The organization onboarding — how needs are articulated and scoped
- The contributor profiles — what verification and credibility signals are visible
- The matching quality — whether suggested contributors align with your actual needs

## Current state

This is a concept-stage prototype built to demonstrate the core product thesis. It includes:

- Conversational AI onboarding for contributors and organizations
- Skill-to-need matching engine
- Task management with full lifecycle tracking
- Impact metrics dashboard (hours contributed, dollar value, completion rates)
- Admin moderation tools
- Organization and contributor verification workflows

The prototype runs on Base44 infrastructure. Production deployment will migrate to a custom stack as the platform scales.
